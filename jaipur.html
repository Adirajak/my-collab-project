Explore Jaipur

The Pink City — Royal palaces, forts, and vibrant culture!

Jaipur Highlights

Nature Places


Jal Mahal

  • Entry Fee: Free (view from outside)
  • Timing: 6am - 6pm
  • Description: A palace in the middle of Man Sagar Lake surrounded by Aravalli hills.

Kanak Vrindavan

  • Entry Fee: ₹20
  • Timing: 9am - 5pm
  • Description: Beautiful garden complex with fountains and Mughal-style architecture.

Nahargarh Biological Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹50
  • Timing: 8:30am - 5:30pm
  • Description: Home to tigers, leopards, and other wildlife with eco-friendly surroundings.

Historical Places


Amber Fort

  • Entry Fee: ₹100
  • Timing: 8am - 5:30pm
  • Description: Majestic fort with hilltop views, elephant rides, and Sheesh Mahal inside.

City Palace

  • Entry Fee: ₹200
  • Timing: 9am - 5pm
  • Description: Royal palace complex blending Mughal and Rajasthani architecture with museums.

Hawa Mahal

  • Entry Fee: ₹50
  • Timing: 9am - 4:30pm
  • Description: Iconic five-story palace with 953 small windows for royal ladies to watch street life.

Malls


World Trade Park

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Premium shopping mall with multiplex, international brands, and food court.

Triton Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Family mall with a variety of shops, eateries, and gaming zones.

Pink Square Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Shopping, food court, and multiplex in Raja Park area.

Restaurants


LMB (Laxmi Mishthan Bhandar)

  • Type: Vegetarian Restaurant & Sweet Shop
  • Timing: 8am - 11pm
  • Description: Famous for Rajasthani thali and sweets since 1954.

Chokhi Dhani

  • Type: Ethnic Village Resort
  • Timing: 5pm - 11pm
  • Description: Rajasthani cultural experience with folk dance, camel rides, and traditional food.

Bar Palladio

  • Type: Italian Restaurant & Bar
  • Timing: 6pm - 11pm
  • Description: Elegant setting with Italian food, cocktails, and royal décor.

Parks


Central Park Jaipur

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 5am - 9pm
  • Description: Green oasis with a musical fountain and walking/jogging track.

Jawahar Circle

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am - 9pm
  • Description: Largest circular park in Asia with musical fountains and rose gardens.

Ram Niwas Garden

  • Entry Fee: ₹10
  • Timing: 9am - 5pm
  • Description: 19th-century garden complex with Albert Hall Museum inside.
