Bhopal Highlights

Nature Places


Upper Lake View

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am - 8pm
  • Description: Scenic boating and beautiful sunset views.

Sheetal Das Ki Bagiya

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 4am - 10pm
  • Description: Lakeside park with boating, gardens, and family picnic spots.

Kerwa Dam

  • Entry Fee: ₹20
  • Timing: 9am - 6pm
  • Description: Picnic spot with ziplining and nature trails.

Historical Places


Sadar Manzil

  • Timing: 10am - 5pm

Malls


DB City Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free

Restaurants


Manohar Dairy

  • Type: Vegetarian Restaurant
  • Timing: 9am - 10pm

Parks


Van Vihar National Park
