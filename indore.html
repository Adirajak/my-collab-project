Explore Indore

The heart of Madhya Pradesh — food, culture, and vibrant city life!

Indore Highlights

Nature Places


Patalpani Waterfall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am - 7pm
  • Description: Majestic waterfall and picnic spot surrounded by lush greenery.

Ralamandal Wildlife Sanctuary

  • Entry Fee: ₹60
  • Timing: 9am - 6:30pm
  • Description: Trekking, wildlife, and panoramic city views.

Pipliyapala Regional Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹25
  • Timing: 11am - 9pm
  • Description: Boating, gardens, musical fountains, and family fun.

Historical Places


Rajwada Palace

  • Entry Fee: ₹20
  • Timing: 10am - 5pm
  • Description: Iconic 18th-century palace blending Maratha and Mughal architecture.

Lal Bagh Palace

  • Entry Fee: ₹10
  • Timing: 10am - 5pm (Closed on Mondays)
  • Description: Grand royal residence with lavish interiors and gardens.

Central Museum Indore

  • Entry Fee: ₹20
  • Timing: 10am – 5pm (Closed on Mondays)
  • Description: One of the oldest museums in Madhya Pradesh, showcasing artifacts, sculptures, and relics of Malwa’s cultural heritage.

Malls


Treasure Island Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Shopping, entertainment, and food court in the city center.

C21 Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11 am – 10 pm
  • Description: Compact shopping destination featuring retail shops, eateries, and a small multiplex cinema.

Phoenix Citadel Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 10 am – 10 pm
  • Description: Large modern mall with international and domestic brands, food courts, family entertainment, and cinema.
