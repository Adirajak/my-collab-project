Explore Bhopal

Discover the City of Lakes — nature, history, and culture await you.

Bhopal Highlights

Nature Places


Upper Lake View

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am - 8pm
  • Description: Scenic boating and beautiful sunset views.

Sheetal Das Ki Bagiya

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 4am - 10pm
  • Description: Lakeside park with boating, gardens, and family picnic spots.

Kerwa Dam

  • Entry Fee: ₹20
  • Timing: 9am - 6pm
  • Description: Picnic spot with ziplining and nature trails.

Historical Places


Sadar Manzil

  • Entry Fee: ₹10
  • Timing: 10am - 5pm
  • Description: Red-brick Nawabi architecture in the old city.

Rani Kamlapati Mahal

  • Entry Fee: ₹20
  • Timing: 9am - 5pm
  • Description: Historic 18th-century palace built by Gond queen Kamlapati, known for its Mughal and regional architecture.

Rani Mahal

  • Entry Fee: ₹15
  • Timing: 10am - 6pm
  • Description: A historic palace known for its elegant architecture and royal history, offering a glimpse into the life of queens of the past.

Malls


DB City Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Major shopping and food destination.

Peoples Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Large shopping and entertainment mall with a food court, multiplex, and amusement park-style attractions.

Aashima Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Modern shopping mall featuring retail brands, dining options, and a multiplex cinema.

Restaurants


Manohar Dairy

  • Type: Vegetarian Restaurant
  • Timing: 9am - 10pm
  • Description: Famous for sweets, chaat, and snacks.

Under the Mango Tree

  • Type: Garden Café & Fine Dining
  • Timing: 12pm – 11pm
  • Description: A serene garden restaurant known for its lush ambience, multi-cuisine menu, and peaceful vibes.

Under the Jamun Tree

  • Type: Rooftop Dining
  • Timing: 1pm – 11pm
  • Description: Stylish rooftop café with rustic charm and an excellent mix of Indian and continental dishes.

Cafechino

  • Type: Café & Bakery
  • Timing: 10am – 10pm
  • Description: Cozy café offering artisan coffee, shakes, Italian snacks, and desserts in a relaxed setting.

Green House Bistro

  • Type: Eco-Themed Café
  • Timing: 11am – 10pm
  • Description: A plant-filled bistro focusing on organic and fresh meals with vegan and vegetarian options.

Winds n Waves

  • Type: Lake View Restaurant
  • Timing: 12pm – 11pm
  • Description: Popular restaurant on the lakefront offering Indian, Mughlai, and Chinese dishes with beautiful sunset views.

Parks


Van Vihar National Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹50
  • Timing: 7am - 5pm
  • Description: Wildlife sanctuary with walking and cycling trails.

Fun Pal Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹30
  • Timing: 10am – 8pm
  • Description: A popular amusement and picnic park with rides, slides, and open green spaces for kids and families.

Chinar Park

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am – 8pm
  • Description: Beautifully maintained garden and walking area with lush greenery and children’s play equipment.

Kamla Nehru Bal Udhyan

  • Entry Fee: ₹20
  • Timing: 8am – 8pm
  • Description: A large children’s park featuring play rides, open lawns, and a family-friendly picnic environment.

Ekant Park

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am – 8pm
  • Description: Calm and peaceful park by the lake, ideal for morning walks, jogging, and relaxation.

Fun City

  • Entry Fee: ₹50
  • Timing: 11am – 8pm
  • Description: Entertainment and amusement park with water rides, thrill rides, and fun activities for all ages.
