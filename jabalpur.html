Explore Jabalpur

City of marble rocks and breathtaking natural wonders.

Jabalpur Highlights

Nature Places


Bhedaghat Marble Rocks

  • Entry Fee: ₹30 for boating
  • Timing: 9am - 6pm
  • Description: Famous for marble cliffs along Narmada river and boat rides.

Dhuandhar Falls

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am - 7pm
  • Description: Stunning waterfall producing a smoke-like mist effect.

Bargi Dam

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 8am - 6pm
  • Description: Scenic spot for boat rides, sunsets, and relaxing picnics.

Historical Places


Madan Mahal Fort

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 8am - 5:30pm
  • Description: Ancient hilltop fort with panoramic views of Jabalpur city.

Chausath Yogini Temple

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 7am - 7pm
  • Description: Ancient 10th-century temple dedicated to 64 Yoginis, known for its circular design and hilltop views near Bhedaghat.

Rani Durgavati Museum

  • Entry Fee: ₹10
  • Timing: 10am - 5pm
  • Description: Museum preserving local history and sculptures of Gondwana kingdom.

Malls


South Avenue Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Shopping, food court and movie screens in the heart of Jabalpur.

Restaurants


Indique

  • Type: Multi-cuisine
  • Timing: 11am - 11pm
  • Description: Popular for rooftop dining and North Indian flavors.

Best Baker & Café

  • Type: Café
  • Timing: 9am - 10pm
  • Description: Cosy bakery serving fresh cakes and coffee.

Parks


Balancing Rock

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 9am - 6pm
  • Description: Natural wonder featuring a huge balancing boulder near Madan Mahal.
