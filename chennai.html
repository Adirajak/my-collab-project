Explore Chennai

The cultural capital of South India — beaches, temples, and heritage charm!

Chennai Highlights

Nature Places


Marina Beach

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 5am - 9pm
  • Description: India’s longest urban beach, bustling with food stalls, pony rides, and sea breeze walks.

Guindy National Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹20
  • Timing: 9am - 5:30pm
  • Description: A green oasis home to blackbucks, deer, birds, and reptiles, right in the heart of Chennai.

Adyar Eco Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹30
  • Timing: 10am - 6pm
  • Description: Restored mangrove ecosystem with nature trails, boating, and environmental education center.

Historical Places


Fort St. George

  • Entry Fee: ₹25
  • Timing: 9am - 5pm (Closed Fridays)
  • Description: The first English fortress in India, built in 1644, housing a museum of colonial history.

Santhome Basilica

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am - 9pm
  • Description: Stunning neo-gothic style church built over the tomb of St. Thomas the Apostle.

Kapaleeshwarar Temple

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 5am - 9pm
  • Description: Dravidian-style temple dedicated to Shiva, dating back to the 7th century with a vibrant gopuram tower.

Parks


Semmozhi Poonga

  • Entry Fee: ₹15
  • Timing: 10am - 7pm
  • Description: Botanical garden with themed gardens, fountains, and rare plant species.

Arignar Anna Zoological Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹100
  • Timing: 9am - 5pm (closed Tuesdays)
  • Description: One of India’s largest zoos with tigers, elephants, lions, and a safari park.

MGM Dizzee World

  • Entry Fee: ₹699 approx
  • Timing: 10:30am - 6:30pm
  • Description: Popular amusement park with thrilling rides, water slides, and a fun zone for kids.

Malls


Phoenix Marketcity

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Chennai’s largest mall with global brands, multiplex, and food courts.

Express Avenue

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 10am - 10pm
  • Description: Huge shopping and entertainment complex in Royapettah with a multiplex and gaming zone.

Vijaya Forum Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Popular mall with shopping, multiplex cinema, and multiple dining options.

Restaurants


Murugan Idli Shop

  • Type: South Indian Vegetarian
  • Timing: 7am - 10pm
  • Description: Famous for soft idlis, chutneys, and filter coffee served in a traditional style.

Annapoorna

  • Type: Vegetarian Thali
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Traditional South Indian meals served on banana leaves, popular among locals.

The Madras Pavilion

  • Type: Multi-cuisine Fine Dining
  • Timing: 12pm - 11pm
  • Description: Luxury dining with global and South Indian specialties in a sophisticated setting.
