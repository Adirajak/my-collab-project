Mumbai Highlights

Nature Places


Sanjay Gandhi National Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹58
  • Timing: 7:30am - 5:30pm (Closed Mondays)
  • Description: Vast green expanse with nature trails, Kanheri caves, and a mini-zoo.

Marine Drive

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: Open 24 hours
  • Description: Iconic 3-km-long promenade along the Arabian Sea, perfect for sunset views.

Hanging Gardens

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 5am - 9pm
  • Description: Terraced gardens with hedges shaped like animals and spectacular city views.

Historical Places


Gateway of India

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: Open 24 hours
  • Description: Mumbai’s landmark archway built during the British Raj, facing the Arabian Sea.

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus

  • Entry Fee: Free (platform ticket required)
  • Timing: Open 24 hours
  • Description: UNESCO-listed Gothic Revival railway station with intricate architecture.

Elephanta Caves

  • Entry Fee: ₹40 (Indians), ₹600 (foreigners)
  • Timing: 9am - 5pm (closed Mondays)
  • Description: Ancient cave temples dedicated to Shiva, located on Elephanta Island.

Parks


Jijamata Udyan (Byculla Zoo)

  • Entry Fee: ₹25
  • Timing: 9am - 6pm (closed Wednesdays)
  • Description: Historic zoo and botanical garden in the heart of South Mumbai.

Shivaji Park

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 5am - 9pm
  • Description: Historic sports ground and community hub in Dadar.

Snow World Mumbai

  • Entry Fee: ₹650 approx
  • Timing: 11am - 9pm
  • Description: Indoor amusement park with ice slides, snow play areas, and snowfall experience.

Malls


Palladium Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Mumbai’s premier luxury shopping destination in Lower Parel.

High Street Phoenix

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Massive shopping and entertainment complex in Lower Parel.

Inorbit Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Popular suburban mall with shopping, restaurants, and a multiplex cinema.

Restaurants


Bademiya

  • Type: Street Kebabs & Grills
  • Timing: 6pm - 2am
  • Description: Legendary kebab and grill stall near Colaba, serving since 1946.

Britannia & Co.

  • Type: Parsi & Iranian
  • Timing: 12pm - 4pm (closed Sundays)
  • Description: Legendary Irani café known for berry pulao and Parsi dishes.

The Table

  • Type: Modern European
  • Timing: 12pm - 11:30pm
  • Description: Award-winning contemporary restaurant with a chic vibe in Colaba.
