Explore Hyderabad

The city of pearls — a blend of heritage, food, and modern lifestyle!

Hyderabad Highlights

Nature Places


Hussain Sagar Lake

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 8am - 10pm
  • Description: Iconic lake with the Buddha statue and boating facilities in the heart of the city.

KBR National Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹20
  • Timing: 5am - 9am, 4pm - 6pm
  • Description: Urban national park with a rich variety of flora and fauna and walking trails.

Shamirpet Lake

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am - 7pm
  • Description: Scenic lake north of Hyderabad, a perfect getaway for picnics and birdwatching.

Historical Places


Charminar

  • Entry Fee: ₹20
  • Timing: 9am - 5:30pm
  • Description: Hyderabad’s grand landmark with four minarets, bustling bazaars, and rich heritage.

Golconda Fort

  • Entry Fee: ₹25
  • Timing: 9am - 5:30pm
  • Description: Magnificent fortress with acoustic architecture and light & sound shows in the evenings.

Qutb Shahi Tombs

  • Entry Fee: ₹10
  • Timing: 9:30am - 4:30pm
  • Description: Historic tombs of Qutb Shahi dynasty kings, with Persian-style domes and gardens.

Parks


Lumbini Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹10
  • Timing: 9am - 9pm
  • Description: Well-maintained urban park with musical fountains and boating views of the Buddha statue.

Kotla Vijayabhaskara Reddy Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹20
  • Timing: 5am - 8pm
  • Description: Green lung with walking and jogging tracks, kids’ play areas, and meditation zones.

Ocean Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹500 approx
  • Timing: 11am - 7:30pm
  • Description: Water rides, amusement zones, and pools perfect for a day of fun with family.

Malls


Inorbit Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Premium mall with top brands, cinema, and multi-cuisine restaurants.

Forum Sujana Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Spacious shopping complex with dining options and entertainment.

GVK One Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Trendy mall with high-end fashion brands and a large aquarium inside.

Restaurants


Paradise Biryani

  • Type: Hyderabadi Biryani
  • Timing: 11am - 11pm
  • Description: Iconic biryani house serving authentic Hyderabadi biryani since 1953.

Chutneys

  • Type: South Indian Vegetarian
  • Timing: 7am - 11pm
  • Description: Renowned for its variety of dosas, idlis, and a wide selection of house-made chutneys served in a family-friendly ambiance.

Bawarchi Restaurant

  • Type: Multi-cuisine
  • Timing: 11am - 11pm
  • Description: Local favorite for generous biryani portions and Mughlai curries.
