Explore Kolkata

The city of joy — rich heritage, art, and a vibrant cultural spirit!

Kolkata Highlights

Nature Places


Eco Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹30
  • Timing: 2pm - 8pm
  • Description: Huge urban park with boating, gardens, and themed zones around a central lake.

Acharya Jagadish Chandra Bose Botanical Garden

  • Entry Fee: ₹10
  • Timing: 10am - 5pm
  • Description: Historical botanical garden famous for the Great Banyan Tree and plant diversity.

Alipore Zoo

  • Entry Fee: ₹30
  • Timing: 9am - 5pm
  • Description: One of India's oldest zoos, housing tigers, elephants, and a reptile house.

Historical Places


Victoria Memorial

  • Entry Fee: ₹30
  • Timing: 10am - 5pm (Closed Mondays)
  • Description: Stunning marble structure with museum galleries and lush gardens.

Indian Museum

  • Entry Fee: ₹50
  • Timing: 10am - 5pm (Closed Mondays)
  • Description: The largest and oldest museum in India, displaying ancient artifacts, fossils, and art.

Dakshineswar Kali Temple

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am - 8pm
  • Description: Famous riverside temple dedicated to Goddess Kali with a spiritual ambiance.

Parks


Millennium Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹10
  • Timing: 10am - 8pm
  • Description: Riverside park on Strand Road with gardens and children’s rides.

Nicco Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹300 approx
  • Timing: 11am - 8pm
  • Description: Popular amusement park with water slides, roller coasters, and games.

Central Park Salt Lake

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am - 8pm
  • Description: Large public park with walking tracks, a lake, and beautiful landscaping.

Malls


South City Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Major shopping mall with big brands, multiplex, and food court.

Acropolis Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Modern mall with a mix of shopping, dining, and entertainment options.

Quest Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Premium mall with luxury brands, restaurants, and a multiplex cinema.

Restaurants


6 Ballygunge Place

  • Type: Bengali Cuisine
  • Timing: 12pm - 10pm
  • Description: Famous for authentic Bengali thalis and seafood specialties.

Peter Cat

  • Type: North Indian & Continental
  • Timing: 12pm - 11pm
  • Description: Iconic restaurant known for Chelo kebabs and retro vibes.

Oh! Calcutta

  • Type: Bengali Fine Dining
  • Timing: 12pm - 11pm
  • Description: Upscale restaurant specializing in traditional Bengali cuisine with elegant décor.
