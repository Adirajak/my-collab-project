Explore Jabalpur

City of marble rocks and breathtaking natural wonders.

Jabalpur Highlights

Nature Places


Bhedaghat Marble Rocks

  • Entry Fee: ₹30 for boating
  • Timing: 9am - 6pm
  • Description: Famous for marble cliffs along Narmada river and boat rides.

Dhuandhar Falls

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am - 7pm
  • Description: Stunning waterfall producing a smoke-like mist effect.

Bargi Dam

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 8am - 6pm
  • Description: Scenic spot for boat rides, sunsets, and relaxing picnics.

Historical Places


Madan Mahal Fort

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 8am - 5:30pm
  • Description: Ancient hilltop fort with panoramic views of Jabalpur city.

Chausath Yogini Temple

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 7am - 7pm
  • Description: Ancient 10th-century temple dedicated to 64 Yoginis, known for its circular design and hilltop views near Bhedaghat.

Rani Durgavati Museum

  • Entry Fee: ₹10
  • Timing: 10am - 5pm
  • Description: Museum preserving local history and sculptures of Gondwana kingdom.

Malls


South Avenue Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Shopping, food court and movie screens in the heart of Jabalpur.

Samdareeya Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Popular shopping mall with branded stores, food court, and multiplex.

Cosmos Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Modern shopping complex with retail outlets, eateries, and entertainment options.

Restaurants


Takht Hazara

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 7am - 6pm
  • Description: Historic site believed to have religious and mythological significance, featuring scenic surroundings and heritage architecture.

70 MM Dining

  • Type: Theme Restaurant
  • Timing: 12pm - 11pm
  • Description: Bollywood-themed family restaurant with multi-cuisine menu and lively décor inspired by Indian cinema.

Rajwada Restaurant

  • Type: Indian Fine Dining
  • Timing: 11am - 11pm
  • Description: Traditional décor and authentic Indian cuisine with a royal ambiance inspired by Rajputana heritage.

Parks


Bharwati Garden

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am – 8pm
  • Description: A serene and well-maintained garden with flowering beds, paved walking paths, and benches for relaxation.

Dumna Nature Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹20 (plus extra for boating)
  • Timing: 7am – 6pm
  • Description: Expansive forested reserve with wildlife safaris, boating, children's area, and nature trails—ideal for family outings.

Balancing Rock

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 9am - 6pm
  • Description: Natural wonder featuring a huge balancing boulder near Madan Mahal.
