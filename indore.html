Explore Indore

The heart of Madhya Pradesh — food, culture, and vibrant city life!

Indore Highlights

Nature Places

Patalpani Waterfall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am - 7pm
  • Description: Majestic waterfall and picnic spot surrounded by lush greenery.

Ralamandal Wildlife Sanctuary

  • Entry Fee: ₹60
  • Timing: 9am - 6:30pm
  • Description: Trekking, wildlife, and panoramic city views.

Pipliyapala Regional Park

  • Entry Fee: ₹25
  • Timing: 11am - 9pm
  • Description: Boating, gardens, musical fountains, and family fun.

Historical Places

Rajwada Palace

  • Entry Fee: ₹20
  • Timing: 10am - 5pm
  • Description: Iconic 18th-century palace blending Maratha and Mughal architecture.

Lal Bagh Palace

  • Entry Fee: ₹10
  • Timing: 10am - 5pm (Closed on Mondays)
  • Description: Grand royal residence with lavish interiors and gardens.

Malls

Treasure Island Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Shopping, entertainment, and food court in the city center.

Restaurants

Sarafa Bazaar

  • Type: Street Food Night Market
  • Timing: 8pm - 2am
  • Description: Legendary for Indori snacks, sweets, and vibrant nightlife.

Chappan Dukan

  • Type: Food Street
  • Timing: 8am - 11pm
  • Description: 56 iconic food stalls with local and international cuisines.

Parks

Meghdoot Garden

  • Entry Fee: ₹20
  • Timing: 6am - 8pm
  • Description: Lush green lawns, fountains, and play areas for families.
