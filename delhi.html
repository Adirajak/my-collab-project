Explore Delhi

India’s capital — a blend of heritage, modernity, food, and culture!

Delhi Highlights

Nature Places


Yamuna Biodiversity Park

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 10am - 5pm
  • Description: 457-acre green zone with native plant species, wetlands, birdwatching, and guided nature walks.

Asola Bhatti Wildlife Sanctuary

  • Entry Fee: ₹40
  • Timing: 9am - 5pm (closed Mondays)
  • Description: Urban wildlife reserve home to blackbucks, birds, butterflies, and serene nature trails.

Sanjay Van

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am - 7pm
  • Description: Urban forest and green lung of Delhi with cycling trails and spots for birdwatching.

Historical Places


Red Fort

  • Entry Fee: ₹35 (Indians), ₹500 (foreigners)
  • Timing: 9am - 5pm (closed Mondays)
  • Description: Iconic 17th-century Mughal fortress with museums and grand architecture.

Qutub Minar

  • Entry Fee: ₹35 (Indians), ₹550 (foreigners)
  • Timing: 7am - 5pm
  • Description: Tallest brick minaret in the world, a UNESCO World Heritage Site with intricate carvings.

India Gate

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: Open 24 hours
  • Description: 42-meter-high war memorial dedicated to Indian soldiers of World War I.

Parks


Lodhi Garden

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 6am - 8pm
  • Description: Historical tombs, landscaped gardens, and walking trails in the heart of Delhi.

Garden of Five Senses

  • Entry Fee: ₹35
  • Timing: 9am - 7pm
  • Description: 20-acre public park with art installations, flower gardens, and amphitheater events.

Nehru Park

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 5am - 9pm
  • Description: Large green park popular for picnics, morning walks, and musical events.

Malls


Select Citywalk

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 10am - 11pm
  • Description: High-end mall in Saket with global brands, food court, and cinema.

Pacific Mall

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Popular shopping mall with many food options, brands, and a multiplex.

DLF Mall of India

  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Timing: 11am - 10pm
  • Description: Massive shopping complex with over 300 brands, food court, and entertainment zone.

Restaurants


Raajsik

  • Type: North Indian & Mughlai
  • Timing: 12pm – 11pm
  • Description: Elegant fine‑dining restaurant known for royal flavors, including kebabs, biryanis, and signature gravies in a regal setting.

Saravana Bhavan

  • Type: South Indian Vegetarian
  • Timing: 8am - 11pm
  • Description: Iconic South Indian restaurant chain known for dosas and thalis.

Bukhara

  • Type: North Indian Fine Dining
  • Timing: 12:30pm - 3pm, 7pm - 11:30pm
  • Description: World-famous restaurant at ITC Maurya, specializing in tandoori dishes.
